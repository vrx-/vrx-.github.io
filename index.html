Biography

I’m a Ph.D. Candidate in Oceanography at Texas A&M University. My research interests include coastal physical-biological interactions, shelf and river plume dynamics, seasonal hypoxia, and the ecological relevance of different scales of variability in the shelf. I use ROMS (COAWST) as a modeling tool, and Python to analyze model output and observational data.
